Rutherfordium is a chemical element. It has also been named ekahafnium and unnilquadium. It has the symbol Rf. It has the atomic number 104. It is a very radioactive element that does not exist in nature. It has to be made. Rutherfordium is the first transactinide element.. The isotope that has the longest halflife is 265Rf. Rutherfordium265 only has a half life of about 13 hours.
