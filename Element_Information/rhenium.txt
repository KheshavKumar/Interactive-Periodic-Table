Rhenium is a chemical element.  It has the chemical symbol Re.  It has the atomic number 75.  It is a rare metal.  It is silver white.  In chemistry it is placed in a group of metal elements called the transition metals.  The chemistry of rhenium is similar to manganese. Discovered in 1908 rhenium was the secondlast stable element to be discovered (stable meaning not radioactive). It was named after the river Rhine in Europe.
